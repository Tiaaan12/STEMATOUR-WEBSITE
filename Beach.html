Doña Choleng Beach Resort

Welcome to Doña Choleng Beach Resort! Located at the Islands of Cagbalete. This beach resort seems to be 
      created exclusively for those who have a deep love for the simplicity of provincial life. This spectacular resort is 
      located right on the beautiful beach, and it has drawn a lot of attention for its unique resort rooms and huts.

Services

These are the few examples of the services we offer

Bali Hut

The Dona Choleng Camping Resort offers a unique and 
      unforgettable accommodation experience with its Bali huts. 
      These traditional huts are crafted from natural materials and 
      offer a rustic charm that blends seamlessly with the resort’s idyllic 
      tropical surroundings. Whether you’re looking for a romantic 
      escape or a fun-filled family adventure, the Bali huts at Dona 
      Choleng Camping Resort provide the perfect base for your 
      beach getaway.

Rooms

The Dona Choleng Camping Resort boasts luxurious rooms that 
      are designed to provide guests with the ultimate in comfort and 
      relaxation. Each room is tastefully decorated with modern 
      amenities and elegant furnishings, offering a warm and inviting 
      atmosphere. With spacious interiors, comfortable beds, and 
      breathtaking views of the surrounding tropical landscape, the 
      rooms at Dona Choleng Camping Resort are the perfect place to 
      escape from the stresses of everyday life.

Tents

The Dona Choleng Camping Resort offers a unique and 
      authentic camping experience with its high-quality camping 
      tents. Set amidst lush tropical foliage, the camping tents provide 
      a comfortable and convenient way to immerse yourself in the 
      natural beauty of Cagbalate Island. Experience the ultimate in 
      outdoor living and create lasting memories at this stunning 
      beach resort.
