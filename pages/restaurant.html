Lutong Bahay and House of Nipaog

Lutong Bahay and House of Nipanog is located on Enverga Street, Bagong Bayan in Mauban. Such delicious delicacies and foods can be found in the restaurant, including atsara, liputi palaman, botart, and more.

Restaurant Details

Location

Located along Enverga Street in Bagong Bayan, Mauban, the restaurant is easy to include in a town tour before or after visiting nearby local spots.

Local Foods

The page highlights Mauban-style home cooking, local delicacies, and familiar Filipino dishes served in a simple and welcoming setting.

Best For

Good for family meals, quick food stops, local food discovery, and visitors who want something more authentic than a regular fast-food stop.
